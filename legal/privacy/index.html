Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Cabinet ("the App") is developed by Eduardo Flores ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what happens to your information when you use Cabinet.

The short version: we don't collect anything. Cabinet was built to keep your data on your device, encrypted, and out of our hands entirely.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not operate a server or backend of any kind for Cabinet. As a result, we do not collect, receive, transmit, or have access to:

  • The contents of any drawer or item you create
  • Any secret values, notes, or images you store in the App
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contact information (unless you voluntarily email us, see Section 5)

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you create in Cabinet — drawers, items, secret values, notes, and images — is stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage. Secret values are encrypted before being written to disk. We do not have a mechanism to access this data, decrypt it, or recover it if you lose your device, since we never receive a copy of it.

3. iCloud Sync (Cabinet+)

Cabinet+ subscribers can enable iCloud sync to keep drawers and items consistent across their own devices. When enabled, your encrypted data is transmitted through Apple's iCloud infrastructure and stored in your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's own privacy policy. We do not operate, host, or have access to this data at any point — it moves directly between your devices through your Apple ID. If you do not subscribe to Cabinet+ or do not enable sync, no data leaves your device.

4. Subscriptions and Payment Information

Cabinet+ subscriptions are billed and processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store your payment card details, billing address, or full purchase history — Apple handles all of that under its own privacy policy. We only receive confirmation from Apple's StoreKit framework that an active subscription entitlement exists, so the App can unlock Cabinet+ features; this confirmation does not include any payment details.

5. Contact

If you email us for support, we will have access to your email address and whatever information you choose to include in that message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry and do not add you to any mailing list or share it with third parties.

Contact: Eduardo Flores — edfloreshz@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

Cabinet is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children, consistent with Section 1 above (we do not collect information from anyone).

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, note them in the App's release notes. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store any of your data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf under privacy regulations such as the GDPR or CCPA — all of your data remains under your own control, on your own device (and, if you choose, in your own iCloud account). You can delete all Cabinet data at any time by deleting drawers/items within the App or by deleting the App itself.

9. Notice for Users in Mexico (Aviso de Privacidad Simplificado)

This section is provided in compliance with Mexico's Federal Law on the Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares, "LFPDPPP").

Responsible party (Responsable): Eduardo Flores, developer of Cabinet, Sonora, Mexico. Contact: edfloreshz@gmail.com.

Personal data collected: As described in Section 1, we do not collect personal data through the App itself. The only personal data we ever receive is your email address and message content, and only if you voluntarily email us for support (Section 5).

Purpose of processing: Any email you send us is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or any secondary purpose, and we do not transfer it to third parties.

ARCO rights: Under the LFPDPPP, you have the right to Access, Rectify, Cancel, or Object (Acceso, Rectificación, Cancelación, Oposición — ARCO) to the processing of your personal data. Since we only hold data you send us by email, you may exercise these rights at any time simply by emailing edfloreshz@gmail.com and requesting that we access, correct, delete, or stop processing that correspondence. We will respond within a reasonable time.

Data transfers: We do not sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to third parties. If you enable iCloud sync as a Cabinet+ subscriber, your encrypted vault data is transmitted through Apple's infrastructure under Apple's own privacy policy, as described in Section 3 — this is a transfer to Apple as your chosen cloud provider, not a transfer by us for our own purposes.

Changes to this notice: Any changes to this Aviso de Privacidad Simplificado will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
